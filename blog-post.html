About the Author

John Renaldi is a professor at Northwestern University's Segal Design Institute (McCormick School of Engineering) where he teaches applied AI and product development in the MMM and MPD programs. Through his consultancy Presto Consulting, he helps product, design, and engineering teams at companies like Life360, Realtor.com, and PayNearMe integrate AI into their product development process. Previously, he founded Jiobit (acquired by Life360) and was an executive at Google and Motorola.
