About the Author

John Renaldi is a professor at Northwestern University's Segal Design Institute where he teaches applied AI and product development. Through his consultancy Presto Consulting, he helps product, design, and engineering teams at companies like Life360, Realtor.com, and PayNearMe integrate AI into their product development process. Previously, he founded Jiobit (acquired by Life360) and held product roles at Google and Motorola.
