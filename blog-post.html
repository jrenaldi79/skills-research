John Renaldi is an adjunct professor at Northwestern University's Segal Design Institute and the University of Illinois, where he teaches applied AI and product development. Through Presto Consulting, he advises product and engineering teams at Life360, Realtor.com, and PayNearMe on integrating AI into their workflows. He's a founding member of Illini Angels and an active angel investor. Previously, he founded Jiobit (acquired by Life360) and led product teams at Google and Motorola.
